Fish Taco

Description

This is a recipe where we create a fish taco for your next dinner

Ingredients

  • Cod
  • Olive Oil
  • Taco Shell
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Fry cod in a pan with olive oil
  2. Fry until the cod is done cooking
  3. Place the fish into the taco shell
  4. Season the taco with seasoning
  5. Enjoy!
