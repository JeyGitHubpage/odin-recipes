Tuna Fish Salad

Description

This is a recipe where we create a tuna fish salad for your next lunch

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Olive Oil
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Fry tuna in a pan with olive oil
  2. Fry until the tuna is done cooking
  3. Shred the fish with two forks
  4. Toss the fish with the lettuce
  5. Season the salad with seasoning
  6. Enjoy!
