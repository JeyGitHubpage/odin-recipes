Salmon Cake

Description

This is a recipe where we create a salsmon cake for your next dinner

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Fry salmon in a pan with olive oil
  2. Fry until the salmon is done cooking
  3. Smash the fish into a patty
  4. Season the salmon with seasoning
  5. Enjoy!
